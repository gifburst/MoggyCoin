How are MoggyCoins Laid out?
ver: MoggyCoin version 1
val: Value of the MoggyCoin
date: The time that the message was sent, in the format YYMMDD[hhmm[ss].
sig: Your chosen signature to validate the coin in your name (if you have recived a coin change the sig and key to yours)
key: Your key is a sort of pin to verify its you
rand: String of random characters, encoded in base-64 format.
counter: Binary counter, encoded in base-64 format.
